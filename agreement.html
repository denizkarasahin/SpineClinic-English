Key Terms — General Summary

▾
⚠ This summary is a draft and has no legal binding. Term descriptions can be edited by clicking; changes are saved in the browser.
Investor Agreement
Investment Amount — Updated from the Investor page
€150,000
Investment Instrument
Open for negotiation: common shares, preferred shares, or convertible debt (SAFE). The preferred structure and ratio will be determined together with the investor profile.
Control Structure
Deniz Karaşahin or Osteoid Health Technologies Inc. retains majority control (50%+1) under all circumstances. The investor is granted minority shares and information rights.
Dividends
No dividends will be distributed until the company reaches cumulative profitability. After reaching cumulative positive, the decision to distribute from annual net profit rests with the board. Osteoid Inc. reserves the right to issue VAT-exempt invoices as software license or royalty fees for completed R&D projects; these payments are treated as expenses prior to dividends.
Reporting
Financial discipline is maintained through a Certified Public Accountant (CPA). Monthly income/expense report, quarterly KPI presentation, annual audit. CPA costs are tracked as a separate line item in periodic expenses.
Share Transfer & Right of First Refusal
Any partner wishing to transfer their shares must first offer them to the existing partners. Existing partners may exercise their right of first refusal within 30 days under the same terms.
Exit Option
From the opening date of the second center, a sale to a strategic buyer or the exercise of right of first refusal by an existing partner becomes available. The valuation method is determined by a pre-agreed multiple.
Non-Compete Clause
During the term of the agreement, investment in or participation in competing entities operating in the spinal orthosis field is prohibited. The non-compete clause becomes void upon termination of the agreement.
Technology Supply Guarantee
Osteoid Inc. commits to providing Osteoid Centers with uninterrupted access to brace design software, production materials, and machine maintenance services for as long as the center is open. Should access to technology be disrupted for any reason, Osteoid Inc. shall incur a compensation liability; the duration and amount shall be negotiated by the parties. Furthermore, Osteoid Inc. commits to not providing the same technology to any other client within the city where the center is established, within the center's operational scope.
Doctor Commission Agreement
Since doctors directly referring patients to a center they co-own creates a grey area, a frontman structure will be used. Shares are held under a designated legal/natural person, not in the doctor's name.
Commission Rate & Accrual — Updated from the Market page
25%
The amount is not paid out as direct cash; it accrues as a separate line item in the company's accounts under the doctor's name. The commission is not contractually binding; it is fulfilled upon a usage request.
Use via Expenditure
The accrued commission is covered directly by the company upon request for professional expenses accepted by the medical chamber, such as conference registration, accommodation, books, and publications. Proof of expenditure is required.
Cash Request & Invoicing
If a cash payment is requested, the doctor or a recommended third party must issue an invoice to the company. The invoice must include VAT; payment will be made within 15 business days from the invoice date.
Channel Share — Equity Conversion Right
Deniz Aktan and Tunay Şanlı have the right to use their accrued channel shares, in exchange for establishing and sustaining the referral channel, to purchase shares in the company at a discounted price at a later stage. Conversion terms (price discount, duration, minimum accrued share amount) shall be determined by separate mutual agreement between the parties.
Agreement Duration
12 months; automatic renewal provided both parties give 30 days' written notice. Terms may be renegotiated at the end of the first year.
Confidentiality
Patient data is protected under PDPL (Personal Data Protection Law). Technology infrastructure, pricing, and business processes shall remain confidential during and after the term of the agreement.
Termination
Immediate termination for cause (ethical violation, PDPL violation). Otherwise, 30 days' written notice; pending commission payments shall be settled.
Non-Compete Clause
During the term of the agreement, active roles in competing clinical entities in the spinal orthosis field are prohibited. The non-compete clause becomes void upon termination of the agreement.
