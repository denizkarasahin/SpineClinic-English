Offsets — Royalty & Commission Double-Count Correction

▾
Osteoid extracts €75/brace royalty + €50/brace cutting fee from the clinic in perpetuity. Doctors extract 25% commission. These are separate cash streams already captured in the financial model. Without an offset, a party would receive both equity and a cash stream based on the same underlying profit — double-dipping. The offset reduces their equity claim by the PV of what they already receive in cash.
€73K
PV of (€75 + €50) × annual braces × plan years — reference: Year 1 ≈ €43K at 345 braces
100%
