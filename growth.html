Version April 2026 Prepared by Osteoid Health Technologies Inc.
Why fast scaling is possible? Orthosis and prosthesis centers in Turkey are structurally unable to scale — the process is dependent on human labor, each center must be built from scratch, and quality cannot be standardized. Osteoid's software, 3D manufacturing, and AI technologies remove this constraint: opening a new center is not building a system from scratch, but copying an existing system to a new location. This makes chain expansion genuinely possible in this sector for the first time.

Implementation Timeline
Trigger-Based Roadmap
Month 1–6 · Year 1
Center 1 opening — Istanbul Flagship
License, team, first doctor activations. Standard brace focused. Intern joins at Month 3. Perforated and sensor brace launch at Month 6.
Month 7–11 · Year 1
Product range expanding, break-even approaching
Sensor + perforated brace entering the mix. Diabetic foot and cranial helmet first cases. Break-even ~Month 9. Cumulative positive ~Month 10–11.
Month 9–11 · Trigger
4-month moving average net profit → 2nd center signal
4-month moving average filters out patient fluctuations. When trigger fires, Center 2 location and partner process begins.
Month 13–15 · Early Year 2
Center 2 opening — Ankara or Izmir
Faster ramp using Center 1's learned processes. Local partner brings doctor referral channel.
Year 4–5
Platform maturation — exit readiness
Both centers at full capacity. 3rd center evaluation. Platform at full market penetration — exit valuation basis.
