Why fast scaling is possible? Orthosis and prosthesis centers in Turkey are structurally unable to scale — the process is dependent on human labor, each center must be built from scratch, and quality cannot be standardized. Osteoid's software, 3D manufacturing, and AI technologies remove this constraint: opening a new center is not building a system from scratch, but copying an existing system to a new location. This makes chain expansion genuinely possible in this sector for the first time.

Hub-and-Spoke — Network-Wide Term
5
Charged to every active satellite on 100% of its gross revenue, before the ownership split below — the flagship earns this fee regardless of its equity %, and bears it only pro-rata to that same %. Related-party management fees require transfer-pricing documentation (emsallere uygunluk) and are VAT-able services (unlike KDV-exempt orthosis sales) — flagged for the tax advisor, not modeled here.
⚠ Structure assumption — confirm with tax advisor, not tax advice. Subsidiary Ltd. vs. Branch (şube), per satellite: Turkey has no group taxation — a subsidiary's startup losses are trapped inside its own Ltd. and can never offset the flagship's profit (only that Ltd.'s own future profit, 5-yr carryforward), while a branch's losses flow directly into the flagship's own P&L and reduce its taxable profit immediately. The trade-off: a branch cannot take local investors and does not isolate liability from the flagship, while a subsidiary can raise local capital and keeps satellite-level risk out of the flagship entity. Subsidiary is the default below; switch a satellite to Branch to see its full P&L (fee replaced by "n/a — internal", no minority interest) consolidate into the flagship figures. Entity structure and branch/subsidiary selection require YMM sign-off before execution.

Growth Logic
◎
2nd Center Trigger
When Center 1's 4-month moving average net profit turns positive — the 2nd center setup process begins.
—
Every center uses the same infrastructure: Osteoid Inc.'s software, machinery, protocols. Centers 2 and 3 are not repetitions of Center 1 — they are accelerated versions of learned processes. Marginal setup cost decreases, ramp time shortens.

Faster ramp using Center 1's learned processes. Local partner brings doctor referral channel.

Both centers at full capacity. 3rd center evaluation. Platform at full market penetration — exit valuation basis.
