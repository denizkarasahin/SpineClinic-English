📊 Patient Data — Value Multiplier
Longitudinal patient data accumulated from sensor braces (compliance rate, brace efficacy, treatment outcome) constitutes an independent value asset for strategic acquirers. Real-world clinical data at this scale does not exist in the European market. The academic publication infrastructure validates data quality and methodology externally.

Multiplier effect: Proven clinical database + peer-reviewed publications can lift EV/EBITDA by +1x to +3x — especially when the acquirer is a European distribution partner or medical device company. Osteoid's European bridge via KIMedix makes this scenario concrete.

Note: Patient data valuation is subject to PDPL/GDPR compliance and database structure. This line does not constitute a commitment; it illustrates strategic potential.
Network Structure — Flagship & Satellites
Osteoid scales hub-and-spoke: Istanbul is the flagship — it runs its own clinic and acts as the network's management company. Izmir, Ankara, and future cities are satellites: separate companies, majority-owned by the flagship, each with its own local investors and its own regulatory license. Satellites pay the flagship a management fee on gross revenue, and the flagship holds an equity stake in each satellite's remaining profit — but satellite expansion is funded by local investors at the satellite level, so the flagship's own cap table never dilutes as the network grows. Key Terms — General Summary

▾
⚠ This summary is a draft and has no legal binding. Clause descriptions can be edited by clicking; changes are saved in the browser.
Investor Agreement
Investment Amount — Updated from the Investor tab
€150.000
Investment Instrument
Open to negotiation: ordinary shares, preferred shares, or convertible debt (SAFE). The preferred structure and ratio will be determined together with the investor profile.
Control Structure
Deniz Karaşahin or Osteoid Health Technologies Inc. retains majority control (50%+1) under all circumstances. The investor is granted minority shares and information rights.
Dividends
No dividends will be distributed until the company reaches cumulative positive. After cumulative positive, the distribution decision on annual net profit belongs to the board of directors. Osteoid Inc. retains the right to issue invoices for software licenses or royalties without VAT based on completed R&D projects; these payments are processed as expenses before dividends.
Reporting
Financial discipline is maintained through a Certified Public Accountant (CPA). Monthly income/expense report, quarterly KPI presentation, annual audit. CPA costs are tracked as a separate line item in periodic expenses.
Share Transfer & Right of First Refusal
When any partner wishes to transfer shares, they must first offer them to existing partners. Existing partners may exercise their right of first refusal within 30 days under the same terms.
Exit Option
From the opening date of the second center, sale to a strategic acquirer or right of first refusal for an existing partner may be exercised. The valuation method is determined by a pre-agreed multiple.
Non-Compete
Investment in or participation in competing entities operating in the spinal orthosis field is prohibited for the duration of the agreement. The non-compete clause is void upon termination of the agreement.
Technology Supply Guarantee
Osteoid Inc. guarantees uninterrupted access to brace design software, production materials, and machine maintenance services for Osteoid Centers for as long as the center is operational. Should technology access be interrupted for any reason, Osteoid Inc. incurs liability for compensation; the duration and amount shall be negotiated by the parties. Furthermore, Osteoid Inc. commits not to provide the same technology to any other client within the center's operating area in the same city.
Doctor Commission Agreement
Since doctors directly referring patients to a center they are partners in creates a grey area, a frontman structure will be used. Shares are held through a designated legal/natural person, not in the doctor's own name.
Commission Rate & Accrual — Updated from the Market page
25%
The amount is not paid directly as cash; it accrues in the company account as a separate line item in the doctor's name. Commission is not contractually bound; it is fulfilled upon request for use.
Use via Expense Reimbursement
Accrued commission is covered directly by the company upon request for professional expenses recognized by the medical association, such as congress registration, accommodation, books, and publications. Proof of expense is required.
Cash Request & Invoicing
If a cash payment is requested, the doctor or their designated third party is required to issue an invoice to the company. The invoice is issued inclusive of VAT; payment is made within 15 business days from the invoice date.
Channel Fee — Equity Conversion Right
Deniz Aktan and Tunay Şanlı have the right to use their accrued channel fees — earned for establishing and maintaining the referral channel — to purchase shares in the company at a discounted price in the future. Conversion terms (discount rate, timeline, minimum accrued amount) are determined by a separate agreement between the parties.
Agreement Term
12 months; automatic renewal provided both parties give written notice 30 days in advance. Terms may be renegotiated at the end of the first year.
Confidentiality
Patient data is protected under PDPL (Personal Data Protection Law). Technology infrastructure, pricing, and business processes remain confidential during and after the agreement term.
Termination
Immediate termination for cause (ethical violation, PDPL violation). In other cases, 30 days' written notice required; pending commission payments are settled.
Non-Compete
Active roles in competing clinic structures in the spinal orthosis field are prohibited for the duration of the agreement. The non-compete clause is void upon termination of the agreement.
